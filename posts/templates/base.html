About:

Story Book is a photo-sharing app that aims to share visual tales as told through the lenses of a camera.

As a user, you can view more information about the a post by clicking on the image. You can also go return to the home-page any time by clicking on the 'Story Book' logo above. Lastly, you can filter the posts by location below or search for a specific category through the search form at the top right corner (or under the navbar for mobile users)
